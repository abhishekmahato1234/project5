IN THE CHITWAN NATIONAL PARK

Chitwan National Park in Nepal is a UNESCO World Heritage site renowned as one of the best wildlife-viewing national parks in Asia. A luxury 5-star resort in Chitwan National Park framed by this majestic landscape, Meghauli Serai, A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park surrounds you with stunning natural beauty, rich Tharu culture and unforgettable wildlife adventures. Guests arriving at the hotel are captivated by our contemporary architecture seamlessly blending into the natural setting, in one of the most fascinating jungle safari lodges in Meghauli.

Experiences

The hotel’s setting on the banks of the River Rapti provides panoramic views of endangered Greater One-Horned Rhinoceroses and gharials (crocodiles) set against the backdrop of the lower Himalayan ranges. Exhilarating holistic wildlife activities and tours – including walking safaris, elephant feeding and bathing, canoeing and jeep safaris – are conducted by Nepal's finest naturalist guides. Additional curated experiences include bonnet breakfasts inside the park, indulgent spa treatments by the Lotus Pond, tours of the local Tharu Village and more. Of course, lounging by the cool ripples of the Infinity Pool might be all the adventure you seek. At every moment, our trained staff of hospitality professionals and naturalists are available to help you make the most of your visit to Nepal.

Local Area

Meghauli Serai, A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park is located beside the River Rapti at the gateway to the park, offering guests an abundance of unforgettable places to visit and things to do in Chitwan National Park. The staff at our 5-star resort feature trained hospitality experts and naturalists, who are available to help you get the most out of your visit to this magnificent region. Once back at the resort, we welcome you to enjoy a relaxing dip in the shimmering infinity pool or sip cocktails at the outdoor deck restaurant, enjoying the gentle sounds of the flowing river.
