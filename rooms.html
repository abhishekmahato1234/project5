Our Accommodation Selection

as you step into your hotel room, suite or villa at Meghauli Serai, A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park, you sense the ancient silence and tranquillity of Nepal’s most famous jungle park enveloping you. Stunningly unique, the accommodation at our 5-star hotel resort offers one of the most immersive and authentic ways to experience the jungle wilderness, exotic wildlife and rich Tharu culture of Chitwan National Park.

Travelers seeking an escape from modern bustle and distractions can revel in the privacy and seclusion of our jungle surroundings. Designed specifically for wildlife enthusiasts, the hotel features 16 Luxury Rapti Pool Villas, 12 Deluxe Meghauli Rooms and 1 plush Rapti Mahal Presidential Suite. The walls of each room are adorned with floor-to-ceiling traditional Tharu sketches, each uniquely crafted by a village artist to evoke the true Meghauli character of our setting. Guest comforts such as pillow-top beds with plush bedding, fluffy bathrobes and slippers, room service, and minibars offer a touch of contemporary luxury into our safari wildlife lodge.

Meghauli Deluxe Rooms

Measuring 42.73 square metres, these contemporary rooms showcase interiors with rhino motifs that open onto balconies overlooking the Himalayas and the Rapti River or Terai grasslands below.

Luxury Rapti Pool Villas

Drenched in pastel hues, these 103 -square-metre villas create a calming sanctuary in the heart of Chitwan National Park. Each luxury villa features a private plunge pool and a spacious bedroom with sweeping views of the River Rapti and the National Park. The bedroom opens onto an outdoor lounging nook with uninterrupted views of the Rapti River, offering a splendid spot for a cup of coffee, tea or evening cocktail. Luxurious five-fixture bathrooms are adorned with traditional Nepalese stone spouts..

Presidential Suite – The Rapti Mahal

step inside our grandest accommodation and be greeted by spectacular views of the Rapti River and a private viewing deck extending down to the river. Comprising of two bedrooms, a separate dining and living room, a fully equipped kitchen and a nanny’s room, this 191-square-metre villa is perfect for a family or group holiday. The quarter-lap plunge pool and sun loungers allow for days of relaxation under brilliant skies. In-room spa treatments are also available for Presidential Suite guests.
